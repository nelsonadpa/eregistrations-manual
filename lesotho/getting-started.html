Getting Started

Create your account and verify your identity to access all OBFC services.

Create Account →

Table of Contents

1 Create Your Account 2 Verify Your Email 3 Verify Your Identity (KYC) 4 Your Dashboard

1. Create Your Account

You only need to do this once. Your account gives you access to all OBFC services.

  1. 1

    Visit the OBFC Platform

    Go to services.businessregistrations.gov.ls. Click "Register" to create your account.

  2. 2

    Fill In Your Details

    Enter your first name, surname, national ID number, email address, and a secure password. Then click "Register".

2. Verify Your Email

Before you can log in, you must confirm your email address.

  1. 1

    Check Your Inbox

    Open your email inbox and look for a verification message sent from OBFC.

    Check your email inbox for the verification link
  2. 2

    Click the Verification Link

    Click the link in the email to confirm your account and activate your access.

Can't find the email?

Check your spam or junk folder. If you still don't see it, contact info@obfc.gov.ls for assistance.

3. Verify Your Identity (KYC)

Identity verification is required before you can submit any application. This is a one-time process.

  1. 1

    Upload Your ID Document

    Upload a clear photo of your national ID card or passport.

  2. 2

    Wait for AI Verification

    The system will automatically verify your identity using AI. Wait for the confirmation message before continuing.

4. Your Dashboard

Once your identity is verified, your dashboard is ready. This is where everything begins.

  1. 1

    Welcome to Your Dashboard

    After verification, you will see your dashboard — your home for all OBFC services.

  2. 2

    Start a Service

    To begin any service, click on it from the dashboard. Each service has its own manual with step-by-step guidance.

You're ready

Account setup is complete. You will not need to repeat these steps. Return to the manual index to find the guide for your specific service.
